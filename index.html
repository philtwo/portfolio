About Me

Hey, I'm Philip! I’m a software developer. I enjoy 3d design, creating games, working with firmware, and building web applications. My goal is to be a versatile developer with a wide range of skills, always learning and adapting to new challenges.

What drives me is the opportunity to craft unique, memorable experiences while constantly evolving my work to stay fresh and relevant. I believe in learning through doing, and I’m always eager to tackle challenges that push me to grow as both a developer and a problem solver.

I’m excited to continue growing as a developer, honing my coding skills to evolve both my life and that of those around me.
