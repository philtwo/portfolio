About Me

Hey, I'm Philip! I’m a software developer with a great interest in technology. I’m excited by the endless possibilities in tech, and enjoy developing software, creating games, working with firmware, and building web applications. My goal is to be a versatile developer with a wide range of skills, always learning and adapting to new challenges.

What drives me is the opportunity to craft unique, memorable experiences while constantly evolving my work to stay fresh and relevant. I believe in learning through doing, and I’m always eager to tackle challenges that push me to grow as both a developer and a problem solver.

Outside of coding, I’m a game enthusiast, and someone who loves to solder electronics and cook. I've spent a good portion of my life as a line cook, working with some of the best people I’ve ever met; those experiences have shaped me into who I am today.

I’m excited to continue growing as a developer and explore where this journey takes me, embracing new opportunities as they come.
